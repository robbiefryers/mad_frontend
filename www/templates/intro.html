Welcome to the Maryhill Activity Directory App!

Swipe to the left to take the tutorial!

Or if you prefer you can skip and go straight to the app.

Activity List

The first thing you'll see is the activities list. It shows all the activites going on right now in Maryhill!
The main page only shows the key information. If you want to find out more, just tap on any activity in the list to get more details.

Finding Activities

Find activities easily. Tap the search bar to bring up the keyboard and search for an activity by name.
Not sure what you're looking for yet? No problem, tap the filter button next to the search bar to bring up a list of filter options.

Filters

You can filter activities using any cobination of days, catergory, times and ages. Select all your choices and press OK to see all activities that match your search, easy!

Detailed Info

Once you find the activity you're looking for just tap it to get more information. The symbols are explained below.
The venue of the current activity. Tap the address to open in Google maps.
Contact name of the activity organiser.
Contact phone number of the activity organiser.
Contact email address of the activity organiser.
Any special futher information about the activity, such as if it's only on fortnightly or if its a ladies only event etc.
A list of the days and times the activity is running.
The age ranges that the activity is suitable for.
